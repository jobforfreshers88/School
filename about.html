About The Ray of Hope International

The Ray of Hope International School strives to create a warm and stimulating environment where learning is fun. We engage in activities that promote and extend children's curiosity and awareness of their world. Our primary focus is learning through play and exploration. We encourage children to grow in confidence, self-esteem and independence. Apart from conveying of essential knowledge and skills for living, the priceless ability and confidence to study, understand and apply any subject, and an environment in which children are encouraged to communicate freely and confidently, at the Ray of Hope International school we strive to ensure that all students are happy throughout their time at the school and graduate, feeling enthusiastic about life, having developed new friendships and gained a sense of new achievement in an ethical, highly productive and motivated environment.

Our Aim

* To provide a warm welcoming environment where every child matters

* To ensure children to be safe and secure in our care

* To enable children to form positive and trusting relationships with their peers and with adults

* To provide a range of learning opportunities which support the development of each child individually.

* To create healthy lifestyle to students and parents

*To develop the potential of the children for a bright future.

Our Vision Our vision is to provide quality education in a safe, caring and happy environment and to play an active role in building up the children as responsible citizens with high values in life
Our Misssion The mission of Ray of Hope International School is to cultivate a community of families and teachers working together to provide a safe, stimulating and positive environment that enhances and fosters the physical, emotional, cognitive, creative and social development of individual children through play based learning and discovery.
